Shonen Manga

One Piece

Author: Eiichiro Oda

Published: 1997-Present

Volumes: 100+

Follow Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew in search of the ultimate treasure, the One Piece.

Naruto

Author: Masashi Kishimoto

Published: 1999-2014

Volumes: 72

The story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming Hokage.

Dragon Ball

Author: Akira Toriyama

Published: 1984-1995

Volumes: 42

Follow Goku from childhood to adulthood as he trains in martial arts and explores the world in search of the Dragon Balls.

Seinen Manga

Berserk

Author: Kentaro Miura

Published: 1989-2021

Volumes: 41

The dark fantasy story of Guts, a lone mercenary, and his quest for revenge in a medieval-inspired world.

Baki Hanma

Author: Keisuke Itagaki

Published: 1991-1999 (Original Series)

Volumes: 42

The story of Baki Hanma as he trains and fights to surpass his father, Yujiro Hanma, the strongest creature on Earth.

Fantasy Manga

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Author: Koyoharu Gotouge

Published: 2016-2020

Volumes: 23

Tanjiro Kamado becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Author: Gege Akutami

Published: 2018-Present

Volumes: 20+

Yuji Itadori swallows a cursed object and becomes host to Ryomen Sukuna, a powerful curse, while attending Jujutsu High.

Bleach

Author: Tite Kubo

Published: 2001-2016

Volumes: 74

Ichigo Kurosaki becomes a Soul Reaper and defends humans from evil spirits while guiding departed souls to the afterlife.

Horror/Action Manga

Attack on Titan

Author: Hajime Isayama

Published: 2009-2021

Volumes: 34

Humanity fights for survival against man-eating Titans in this dark fantasy series following Eren Yeager.

Solo Leveling

Author: Chugong (original novel), Jang Sung-rak (artist)

Published: 2018-2020

Volumes: 14

Weakest hunter Sung Jin-Woo gains the ability to level up and becomes one of the most powerful hunters in this webtoon adaptation.
